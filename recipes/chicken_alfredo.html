Chicken Alfredo

A simple pasta dish that uses alfredo sauce, chicken thigh/breast, and broccoli. Can be easily modified to add ingredients.

  • 1 package pasta
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 package cream cheese
  • 2 cups milk
  • 6 ounces parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  1. Bring large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and stir until tender. Drain.
  2. Melt butter in saucepan over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and saute for about 30 seconds.
  3. Stir in cream cheese until smooth and add milk while stirring. Lower heat to medium and add the parmesan cheese, parsley, Italian seasoning, 1/2 tsp salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 5 minutes and remove from heat
  4. Heat olive oil on another pan. Season chicken with salt and garlic powder. Cook chicken for about 6 minutes each side or until center is not pink. Cut into cubes.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Butter baking pan and fill in repeating layers of pasta, chicken, and sauce. Sprinkle top with parmesan cheese and cover and bake.
  6. Bake for about 15 minutes then remove cover and bake for 5 minutes more.
