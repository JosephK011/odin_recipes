Fried Rice

Simple and easy dish that can be customized with a multitude ingredients. This dish with cooked rice and vegetables cannot go wrong.

  • 2/3 cups of chopped carrots
  • 2 tbs vegetable oil
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups cooked and cold white rice
  • 1 tbs soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  1. Place carrots and any other vegetables in small saucepan and cover with water. Cook for 3-5 minutes and drain water.
  2. Cook eggs in a seperate pan scrambled and set aside.
  3. Cook any other protein seperately.
  4. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil and stir in the veggies. Cook for about 30 seconds and add eggs and add any additional protein.
  5. Stir in the rice and add soy sauce. Toss the rice to ensure the rice is coated. Adding oyster sauce here also adds to the flavor. Drizzle with sesame oil and keep tossing the rice.
