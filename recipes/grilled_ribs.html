Grilled Ribs

Tender beef short ribs grilled with barbeque sauce.
  • 1 tbs brown sugar
  • 1 tbs paprika
  • 1 tbs chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 4 pounds beef short ribs
  • Aluminum foil
  • 1/2 cup barbeque sauce
  1. Mix brown sugar, paprika, chili powder, salt, and garlic powder in a bowl.
  2. Cut ribs into equal portions and rub spice mixture onto the ribs. Place ribs in a baking dish and cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. Preheat grill to 375 to 400 degrees.
  4. Cover ribs in aluminum foil packets. Arrange packets on indirect heat on grill. Cover and cook until tender for about 2 hours.
  5. Open packets and brush ribs with barbeque sauce. Move packets back onto grill while turning until browned for about 5 minutes more.
